# V11-07 — Cross-System Review & Delivery Scaling Decision

Status: LATER  
Owner: Executor Agent  
Reviewer: Independent Multi-Perspective Auditor  
Editor: Felix

---

## 1. Mission

Review the whole V1.1 system after evidence, governance, delivery, consolidation and research work to decide whether to scale delivery, research, automation or release hardening next.

---

## 2. Scope

Allowed work:

- Compare V11-01 through V11-06 completion and audit reports.
- Identify systemic failure patterns.
- Determine whether delivery chain is ready to scale.
- Determine whether further research is needed before scaling.
- Recommend release path or additional stabilization.

---

## 3. Non-Scope

Do not:

- perform new research,
- write delivery artifacts,
- alter knowledge objects,
- close release decisions,
- overrule independent audits.

---

## 4. Dependencies

- V11-04 complete.
- V11-05 complete.
- V11-06 complete if executed; otherwise explicitly deferred.

---

## 5. Execution Mode

Autonomous review synthesis. Stop if audits conflict in a way that requires Editor arbitration.

---

## 6. Internal Checkpoints

- Scope Check after artifact inventory.
- State Check against roadmap and completion reports.
- Evidence Check for all scaling claims.
- Pre-Completion Check.

---

## 7. Definition of Done

V11-07 is DONE only when:

1. All prior macro-project outcomes are summarized from source artifacts.
2. Open risks are ranked.
3. Delivery scaling decision options are clear.
4. Research scaling decision options are clear.
5. Automation/integrity scaling decision options are clear.
6. A concrete recommendation for V11-08 readiness is made.
7. Completion report and audit package exist.

---

## 8. Audit Requirements

Audit should verify that the review did not cherry-pick successful outcomes and that unresolved failures are not hidden as deferred work.
