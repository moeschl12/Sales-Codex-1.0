# V11-04 — Early Delivery Vertical Slice

Status: LATER  
Owner: Executor Agent  
Reviewer: Independent Delivery Auditor  
Editor: Felix

---

## 1. Mission

Test whether the existing knowledge architecture can produce high-quality delivery artifacts before expanding the research corpus further.

---

## 2. Scope

Create a narrow end-to-end prototype:

- select one validated topic cluster,
- trace source knowledge objects,
- draft one coherent publication section or chapter fragment,
- create one workbook exercise,
- create one training sequence,
- optionally define a small AI-coach interaction spec,
- document what the knowledge architecture supports and what it lacks.

---

## 3. Non-Scope

Do not:

- write a full book chapter,
- create a full product or app,
- invent new sales techniques not grounded in the repository,
- hide uncertainty for readability,
- perform broad knowledge consolidation.

---

## 4. Dependencies

- V11-01 complete.
- V11-03 complete enough to define validation/delivery rules.
- V11-02 preferred so evidence boundaries are known.

---

## 5. Execution Mode

Autonomous vertical-slice project with strong traceability. Stop if the selected topic lacks enough validated material and no alternative can be selected conservatively.

---

## 6. Internal Checkpoints

- Scope Check after cluster selection.
- Evidence Check before drafting output.
- Delivery Traceability Check after output drafting.
- Health Check before completion.
- Pre-Completion Check.

---

## 7. Definition of Done

V11-04 is DONE only when:

1. Topic cluster selection is justified.
2. Output claims are traceable to knowledge objects, sources or research artifacts.
3. Evidence level and uncertainty are preserved in delivery output.
4. At least one publication fragment, one workbook exercise and one training sequence exist.
5. Gaps are classified: missing examples / missing bridges / missing competencies / weak evidence / unclear audience.
6. A delivery-scaling recommendation is made.
7. Completion report and audit package exist.

---

## 8. Audit Requirements

Audit must verify traceability, didactic quality, evidence discipline and whether delivery scaling is premature.
